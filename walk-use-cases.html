Start Small, Prove Value

These "Walk" use cases are perfect for your first AI agent implementations. Each scenario is designed to be built quickly, demonstrate clear value, and establish a foundation for more complex agents later.

Perfect for MVP Development

Focus on single-purpose agents that solve specific problems with measurable outcomes. These use cases typically require 2-4 weeks to implement and can show immediate ROI.

Customer Support & Service

FAQ Response Agent

Beginner

Automatically answer common customer questions using your existing FAQ database and knowledge base.

What it does:
  • • Receives customer questions via chat, email, or form
  • • Searches internal FAQ and knowledge base
  • • Provides accurate, branded responses
  • • Escalates complex queries to humans
Business impact:
  • • Reduces support team workload by 40-60%
  • • Provides 24/7 instant responses
  • • Improves customer satisfaction scores
  • • Frees agents for complex issues

OutSystems Implementation: Use AI Agent Builder with RAG for FAQ search, integrate with your existing customer portal or chat widget.

Ticket Classification Agent

Beginner

Automatically categorize and route incoming support tickets to the right teams based on content analysis.

What it does:
  • • Analyzes ticket content and attachments
  • • Assigns priority levels (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
  • • Routes to appropriate team/department
  • • Adds relevant tags and metadata
Business impact:
  • • Reduces manual triage time by 80%
  • • Improves first-contact resolution
  • • Ensures urgent issues get priority
  • • Provides consistent categorization

OutSystems Implementation: Use Agent Workbench to integrate with your ticketing system (ServiceNow, Zendesk, etc.) and implement classification logic.

Document & Data Processing

Invoice Data Extraction Agent

Intermediate

Extract key information from invoices, receipts, and financial documents for automatic data entry.

What it does:
  • • Processes PDF, image, and scanned documents
  • • Extracts vendor details, amounts, dates, line items
  • • Validates against business rules
  • • Creates entries in accounting system
Business impact:
  • • Reduces data entry time by 90%
  • • Eliminates manual transcription errors
  • • Speeds up invoice processing
  • • Improves audit trail accuracy

OutSystems Implementation: Use Agent Workbench with OCR capabilities and Data Fabric to connect to your ERP/accounting systems.

Contract Review Assistant

Intermediate

Review contracts and legal documents to identify key terms, risks, and compliance requirements.

What it does:
  • • Scans contracts for standard clauses
  • • Identifies unusual terms and potential risks
  • • Extracts key dates, amounts, and obligations
  • • Flags items requiring legal review
Business impact:
  • • Accelerates contract review process
  • • Reduces legal costs for routine contracts
  • • Ensures consistent risk assessment
  • • Improves contract compliance tracking

OutSystems Implementation: Use AI Agent Builder with legal templates and clause databases. Integrate with document management systems.

Internal Operations

Meeting Summary Agent

Beginner

Generate structured summaries and action items from meeting transcripts and recordings.

What it does:
  • • Processes meeting transcripts or audio files
  • • Identifies key decisions and action items
  • • Extracts participant contributions
  • • Creates formatted meeting notes
Business impact:
  • • Saves 30-45 minutes per meeting
  • • Ensures no action items are missed
  • • Provides consistent meeting documentation
  • • Improves follow-up and accountability

OutSystems Implementation: Use AI Agent Builder to process transcripts from Zoom, Teams, or other meeting platforms. Store summaries in SharePoint or internal portals.

Employee Onboarding Assistant

Beginner

Guide new employees through onboarding tasks, answer HR questions, and track completion progress.

What it does:
  • • Provides step-by-step onboarding guidance
  • • Answers common HR policy questions
  • • Tracks task completion and deadlines
  • • Sends reminders for pending items
Business impact:
  • • Reduces HR workload for routine queries
  • • Improves new hire experience
  • • Ensures consistent onboarding process
  • • Accelerates time-to-productivity

OutSystems Implementation: Use AI Agent Builder with HR knowledge base and integrate with HRIS systems for task tracking and notifications.

Sales & Marketing

Lead Qualification Agent

Intermediate

Score and qualify incoming leads based on predefined criteria and company data.

What it does:
  • • Analyzes lead form submissions and interactions
  • • Assigns lead scores based on qualification criteria
  • • Routes qualified leads to sales teams
  • • Triggers nurturing campaigns for lower scores
Business impact:
  • • Improves sales team efficiency
  • • Increases conversion rates
  • • Reduces time spent on poor leads
  • • Enables faster response times

OutSystems Implementation: Use Agent Workbench to integrate with CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot) and implement scoring algorithms.

Product Recommendation Agent

Intermediate

Suggest relevant products to customers based on their preferences, history, and behavior.

What it does:
  • • Analyzes customer profile and purchase history
  • • Suggests complementary or upgrade products
  • • Provides personalized explanations
  • • Tracks recommendation performance
Business impact:
  • • Increases average order value
  • • Improves customer satisfaction
  • • Reduces product discovery time
  • • Enables personalized experiences

OutSystems Implementation: Use Agent Workbench with customer data from e-commerce platforms and integrate with product catalogs.
